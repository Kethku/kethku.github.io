Short post just to capture that I cut a Neovide release using the instructions that @multisn8 wrote at Neovide.dev for making reproducible releases. Neovide isn't my effort anymore. Its a project owned and lead by the community. I couldn't be more proud.

Neovide 0.10.4 is available now in the release page.
